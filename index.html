Project Summary

1. Initially we get to see a world map loaded for year 2017 HDI value data for all countries.


2. We are using colour hue to compare HDI value of all countries. The lighter the colou the more is the HDI value. And for the countries whose data is unavailable, it is shown in black colour


3. user can hover over any country in map to see its name and HDI value for that year


4. We have added a "NEXT" button to change the year for visualising HDI performance for that particular year


5. If a user click on any country, it will pop up an info panel showing basic info about that country


6. after clicking any country, a donut chart also pops up at bottom, from there you can select any HDI category you want to see, then it will change the vale of the donut chart to sub-categories of the clicked category. Then after clicking the sub-category, a year line chart will open at the right showing the HDI vale on y-axis and year on x-axis.


7. user can compare two countries by clicking on checkbox button at top for comparing two countries. Once he clicks the checkbox, he can simply click on two countries that he wants to compare


8. After that, a donut chart again pops up at bottom, but now, if you click the category it will display a horizontal bar chart at the right side


9. Now if you click the sub-category of the selected category in donut chart, it will display a year line cart for both countries and you can hover over the dots in line chart to view the exact value corresponding to a particular year.


10. You can also select "population" from drop down list above to view population data for all countries. The darker the colour the higher population.
